About us

As soon as it opened its doors in 2011, Meera's Kitchen established itself as a stylish addition to the heart of historic business district of Hoboken, NJ—perfect for lunch, dinner or hanging out in between. It quickly became a local favorite, delighting diners with innovative Indian dishes and re-interpretation of classic American diner fare.
